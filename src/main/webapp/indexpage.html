Cloud Man

Welcome to the CloudMan! It's free App for Managing Files and Transferring Files across Cloud Drives.

With CloudMan you can connect multiple accounts from cloud drives into a single platform. You can easily upload, download, copy and transfer files from all the cloud services you use without having to constantly switch between the different applications.
Once you connect your Cloud Drives, you can manage all your files from a single place.
